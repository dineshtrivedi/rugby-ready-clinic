ABOUT RUGBY READY

The Rugby Ready Clinic is deeply interested in the game of rugby and the individual skills development of young players. The goal is to provide a safe environment for high-performance athletes to further develop their skills in preparation for the 2021 SASRA National weeks.

Offering 1 on 1 skills, conditioning and analysis sessions to our clients to not only help these athletes to be in the best physical shape but also to broaden their understanding of game situations.

# Covid Safety measures

  • All athletes must present a negative covid test. (Dinesh: How often?)
